The Shawshank Redemption

The Riveting Tale of an Innocent Man escaping prison.

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a 1994 movie about Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly sent to Shawshank State Penitentiary for a double murder. Inside, he befriends Red and uses his smarts to help inmates and expose prison corruption. Over time, Andy secretly tunnels his way to freedom, revealing the warden's corruption and his own innocence.
